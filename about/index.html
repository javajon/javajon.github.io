My Passion

I teach many topics to help transform your ideas into applications for your customers. Cloud native has become an excellent delivery channel for your products and features. The ecosystem is large and the path is not always easy. It’s not just polyglot, it’s distributed. It’s expensive. It’s complex.

Throughout my presentations, workshops, writings, and hand-on tutorials these topics are demystified. Sometimes an architecture, a technical stack, or a tool may appear too complex. I love taking these opportunities to break down seemingly complex software mechanisms to show how they work so they can be added to your professional toolbox. When you get to that ah-ha! moment, you feed my passion to teach more.
